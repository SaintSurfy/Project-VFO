The Sinner's Prayer (By Holy Ghost Chapel)

Heavenly Father,
I believe you sent Your son,
Jesus Christ,To die for me,
On the cross of Calvary,
And He rose again,
On the 3rd day,
I repent of all my sins,
And I receive Jesus,
Into my heart,
As my Lord,
And my savior,
Fill me Lord,
With Your Holy Spirit,
Help me to work in holiness,
And righteousness,
In Jesus's Name,
Amen!
